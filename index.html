Power-Up Collector

How To Play:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four power-up items to choose from:
    • Super Mushroom
    • Ice Flower
    • Super Star
    • 1-Up Mushroom
  • By clicking on an item, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each item is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each item.

    • Have fun, and good luck!
